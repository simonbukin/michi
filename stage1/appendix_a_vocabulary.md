# Appendix A — Complete Vocabulary Index

This appendix consolidates every vocabulary item introduced in Chapters 1 through 26 of 日本語の道 Stage 1. Entries are organized alphabetically by あいうえお order of the kana reading.

---

## How to Read This Index

**Columns:**

- **Word** — The word as it appears in the textbook (kanji where applicable).
- **Reading** — The full kana reading. Left blank if the word is already written entirely in kana.
- **Pitch** — Pitch accent using the downstep number system. The circled number indicates the mora after which pitch drops. ⓪ means flat/heiban (no drop). ① means the drop occurs after the first mora. ② after the second, and so on.
- **Part of Speech** — Grammatical category. Verb classes are marked as follows:
  - 五段 = godan / consonant-stem verb
  - 一段 = ichidan / vowel-stem verb
  - する = suru-compound irregular verb
  - くる = kuru irregular verb
  - い-adj = i-adjective
  - な-adj = na-adjective
- **English** — English translation.
- **Ch.** — Chapter of first introduction.

**Sorting:** Entries are sorted by their hiragana reading in standard あいうえお order. Katakana words are sorted by their hiragana equivalent. Long vowels and geminate consonants are treated as separate morae for ordering purposes.

**Notes:** When a single word has multiple distinct meanings introduced in different chapters, the earliest chapter is listed. Counters and their individual number forms (e.g., いっぽん, にほん) are listed selectively; the counter itself is given as the primary entry.
