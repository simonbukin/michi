# Appendix B — Stage 2 Kanji Index

This appendix lists all ~200 new kanji introduced in Stage 2 (Chapters 1–29). Combined with the ~100 kanji from Stage 1, the cumulative total is approximately 300 kanji.

---

## How to Read This Index

**Columns:**

- **Kanji** — The character.
- **音読み** — Sino-Japanese reading(s) in katakana.
- **訓読み** — Native Japanese reading(s) in hiragana. Okurigana are shown after a dot (e.g., かえ.る).
- **Radical** — The primary radical (部首).
- **Meaning** — Core English meaning(s).
- **Example Compounds** — Two or more compound words using this kanji.
- **Ch.** — Chapter of first appearance in Stage 2.

**Notes:** Some kanji may have additional readings not listed here; only the most common readings encountered in Stage 2 are shown. Kanji already introduced in Stage 1 are not repeated.
