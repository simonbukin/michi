# Appendix D — Compound Particle Quick Reference

This appendix organizes all compound particles taught in Stage 3, grouped by function. Each entry includes the formation, meaning, an example, a formality ranking, and the chapter where it was introduced.

---

## How to Read This Reference

**Formality Scale:**

- **5** — Highly formal / written only (academic papers, legal documents)
- **4** — Formal (business writing, news articles, speeches)
- **3** — Semi-formal (business conversation, polite writing)
- **2** — Neutral (general use, both spoken and written)
- **1** — Casual (conversational equivalent exists and is preferred in speech)

**Prenominal Forms:** Many compound particles have a prenominal variant (connecting directly to a noun) using a different ending. These are listed alongside the main form.

## Topic and Scope

These particles mark what something is about, what it concerns, or whose perspective is relevant.

| Particle | Prenominal | Formation | Meaning | Formality | Example | Ch. |
|----------|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| について | についての | Noun + について | about, concerning | 2 | 日本の文化について話す。 | 4 |
| に関して | に関する | Noun + に関して | regarding (more formal) | 4 | 環境問題に関する報告書 | 4 |
| をめぐって | をめぐる | Noun + をめぐって | concerning (implies debate) | 4 | 法案をめぐる議論 | 4 |
| に対して | に対する | Noun + に対して | toward, against, in contrast to | 3 | 学生に対する支援を強化する。 | 4 |
| にとって | にとっての | Noun + にとって | for, from the standpoint of | 2 | 私にとって大切な人だ。 | 4 |

### Usage Notes

- **について** is the most general and versatile. Use it when you simply mean "about."
- **に関して** is a formal upgrade of について. Interchangeable in writing; sounds stiff in casual speech.
- **をめぐって** implies controversy, debate, or multiple perspectives. Do not use it for neutral topics.
- **に対して** has two uses: (1) attitude/action directed toward someone/something, (2) contrast between two things.
- **にとって** marks the person whose perspective is being described. Often followed by evaluative predicates (大切, 重要, 難しい, etc.).

## Basis and Standard

These particles indicate what something is based on, aligned with, or in accordance with.

| Particle | Prenominal | Formation | Meaning | Formality | Example | Ch. |
|----------|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| に基づいて | に基づく | Noun + に基づいて | based on | 4 | 法律に基づいて判断する。 | 5 |
| に応じて | に応じた | Noun + に応じて | depending on, in accordance with | 3 | 能力に応じた給料 | 5 |
| に沿って | に沿った | Noun + に沿って | along, in line with | 3 | 計画に沿って進める。 | 5 |
| をもとに | をもとにした | Noun + をもとに | based on (source material) | 3 | 事実をもとに書かれた小説 | 6 |

### Usage Notes

- **に基づいて** implies a formal or authoritative foundation (law, rule, data, research).
- **に応じて** means the action varies depending on the noun. The result changes as the basis changes.
- **に沿って** means following something linear — a plan, a road, a policy, a river.
- **をもとに** means using something as raw material or source. Often used for creative works based on real events.

## Range and Extent

These particles indicate temporal or spatial scope.

| Particle | Prenominal | Formation | Meaning | Formality | Example | Ch. |
|----------|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| にわたって | にわたる | Noun + にわたって | spanning, over a range | 4 | 三年にわたる研究 | 5 |
| にかけて | — | から Noun にかけて | from X through Y (gradual) | 3 | 春から夏にかけて雨が多い。 | 5 |
| を通じて | — | Noun + を通じて | through, throughout | 3 | 一年を通じて温暖だ。 | 5 |
| を通して | — | Noun + を通して | through (means/medium) | 2 | インターネットを通して情報を得る。 | 5 |

### Usage Notes

- **にわたって** emphasizes the breadth or duration of something. Used for both time (三日間にわたって) and space (広い範囲にわたって).
- **にかけて** always pairs with から and indicates a gradual transition. It implies a fuzzy boundary: "from spring through summer" (not a sharp cutoff).
- **を通じて** and **を通して** are near-synonyms. を通じて leans slightly more toward "throughout an entire period," while を通して leans toward "by means of / via." In practice, they are often interchangeable.

## Formal Locative

| Particle | Prenominal | Formation | Meaning | Formality | Example | Ch. |
|----------|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| において | における | Noun + において | in, at (formal で) | 5 | 国際会議において発表した。 | 5 |

### Usage Notes

- **において** is the formal written equivalent of で. It appears constantly in academic papers, news articles, and official documents.
- In speech, using において sounds very formal or stiff. Use で instead.
- The prenominal form における is extremely common as a modifier: 現代社会における問題 ("problems in modern society").

## Inclusion and Starting Point

These particles indicate what is included, what serves as a starting example, or what is central.

| Particle | Prenominal | Formation | Meaning | Formality | Example | Ch. |
|----------|------------|-----------|---------|-----------|---------|-----|
| をはじめ(として) | — | Noun + をはじめ | starting with, including | 3 | 東京をはじめ大都市で人口が増えている。 | 6 |
| を中心に | を中心とした | Noun + を中心に | centered on | 3 | 駅を中心に商業地区が広がっている。 | 6 |
| に加えて | — | Noun + に加えて | in addition to | 3 | 経験に加えて資格も必要だ。 | 6 |
| はもちろん | — | Noun + はもちろん | of course, not to mention | 2 | 英語はもちろん中国語も話せる。 | 6 |
| のみならず | — | Noun + のみならず | not only (formal) | 5 | 国内のみならず海外でも評価されている。 | 6 |

### Usage Notes

- **をはじめ** names a representative example from a larger group. "Starting with Tokyo" implies other major cities are also included.
- **を中心に** marks what is at the center of an activity, area, or focus.
- **に加えて** is a straightforward "in addition to." Less formal than のみならず.
- **はもちろん** is conversational and natural. It means "X goes without saying, and also Y."
- **のみならず** is the most formal option for "not only." Written Japanese strongly; rarely used in speech.
