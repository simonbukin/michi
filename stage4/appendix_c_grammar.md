# Appendix C — Stage 4 Grammar Index

This appendix lists every grammar point introduced in Stage 4 (Chapters 1–15, 23–24), organized by chapter. Each entry includes the formation rule, meaning, and one example sentence.

---

## How to Read This Index

**Columns:**

- **Grammar Point** — The pattern as it appears in instruction.
- **Formation** — How to construct the pattern from the base form.
- **Meaning** — English translation or functional description.
- **Example** — A model sentence demonstrating the pattern.
- **Ch.** — Chapter of introduction.

**Notes:** Stage 4 focuses on literary, formal, and professional registers. Many patterns originate in classical Japanese or are restricted to written language. Cross-references are provided where patterns interact.
