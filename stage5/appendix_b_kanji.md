# Appendix B — Stage 5 Kanji Index

Stage 5 adds approximately 500 new kanji to the learner's repertoire, bringing the cumulative total to roughly 1,500 characters — well beyond JLPT N1 requirements and into the territory of educated native-level literacy. The kanji in this appendix are organized by semantic cluster, continuing the thematic approach from earlier stages. Stage 5 characters tend toward the literary, historical, philosophical, and specialized domains that distinguish fluent reading ability from mere functional competence.

For each entry: **音** = on'yomi (katakana), **訓** = kun'yomi (hiragana, with okurigana after a dot), **意** = core English meaning, **熟語** = representative compound words.
