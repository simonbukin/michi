# Appendix B — Stage 4 Kanji Index

Stage 4 adds approximately 350 new kanji to the learner's repertoire, bringing the cumulative total to roughly 1,000 characters — the threshold for JLPT N2 and functional literacy in most non-specialist texts. The kanji in this appendix are organized by semantic cluster, following the thematic approach introduced in Chapter 26. Each cluster groups characters that share a conceptual domain, making it easier to learn related vocabulary as interconnected networks rather than isolated symbols.

For each entry: **音** = on'yomi (katakana), **訓** = kun'yomi (hiragana, with okurigana after a dot), **意** = core English meaning, **熟語** = representative compound words.
