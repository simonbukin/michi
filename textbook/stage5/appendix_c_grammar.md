# Appendix C — Stage 5 Grammar Index

This appendix lists every grammar point introduced in Stage 5 (Chapters 1–6), organized by chapter. Each entry includes the formation rule, meaning, and one example sentence.

---

## How to Read This Index

**Columns:**

- **Grammar Point** — The pattern as it appears in instruction.
- **Formation** — How to construct the pattern from the base form.
- **Meaning** — English translation or functional description.
- **Example** — A model sentence demonstrating the pattern.
- **Ch.** — Chapter of introduction.

**Notes:** Stage 5 covers N1-level grammar. These patterns are characteristic of formal writing, literary prose, and elevated speech. Many carry strong emotional or rhetorical force and appear in essays, speeches, and legal or academic documents.
